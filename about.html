Support LGBTQ+ friendly businesses online

As you browse the web, With Pride highlights a business's commitment to LGBTQ+ equality and inclusion with an icon in the browser toolbar. You can tap the icon for a popup with the business's score along with a link to learn more.

Top-scoring business

This icon appears on sites of businesses that have earned a top score of 100

High-scoring business

This icon appears on sites of businesses that have received a score between 70 and 100

Low-scoring business

This icon appears on sites of businesses that have received a score below 70

How are businesses scored?

With Pride utilizes the Human Rights Campaign's "Corporate Equality Index" for business scores. The index is the national benchmarking tool on corporate policies and practices pertinent to LGBTQ+ employees, and is an annual measure of how equitable large private businesses in the United States treat their LGBTQ+ employees, consumers, and investors.

With Pride takes these businesses and matches them to sites, resulting in over 1,300 scored sites, including a majority of the top-visited sites in the US. But there's still lots to do, and we're hard at work continuing to match sites to businesses.

Who's included?

The Human Rights Campaign's "Corporate Equality Index" focuses on corporate policies and practices and therefore includes only large, US businesses. Fortune magazine's 1,000 largest publicly-traded businesses are all invited to participate, and any for-profit, non-government employer with 500 or more full-time US employees can request to participate.
